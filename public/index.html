About Us

From Tsogang Brands to Black62 Commodities

Black62 Commodities is the new identity of Tsogang Brands (Pty) Ltd — a South African trading and bulk logistics business with a decade of operating heritage and over twenty years of combined management experience across mining, energy and agriculture.

Our rebrand reflects our sharper focus: physical commodities trading in coal, chrome and animal feed, supported by our own 62-ton abnormal heavy-haul fleet. We work with reputable producers, hauliers and end-users to move material at scale, on schedule, and with full compliance.

Logistics & Transport

62-Ton Abnormal Heavy-Haul

Our specialised 62-ton abnormal road freight fleet is purpose-built to move bulk coal, chrome and oversized commodity loads across South Africa and the SADC region — with full permit compliance and route management.

  • 62-ton abnormal heavy-haul road freight, with permit and escort coordination
  • Cross-border SADC movements with full documentation
  • Fleet management, dispatch and turnaround scheduling
  • Weighbridge tickets, seals and chain-of-custody on every load
